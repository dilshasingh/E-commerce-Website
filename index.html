About Us

Welcome to Shoppy!

At Shoppy, we're dedicated to bringing you the finest textiles from around the world, right to your doorstep. Our passion for quality fabrics and exceptional customer service drives everything we do, ensuring you have an unparalleled shopping experience.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Shoppy was born out of a love for textiles and a desire to make high-quality fabrics accessible to everyone. What started as a small venture has grown into a thriving online platform, connecting textile enthusiasts, fashion designers, and DIY hobbyists with the materials they need to create their masterpieces.
